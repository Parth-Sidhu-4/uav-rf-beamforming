# Contributing Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in this project. 

## Closed Academic/Proprietary Project

This repository hosts an academic and proprietary research project conducted by **Parth Sidhu** during a summer internship at **Encap Technologies India Private Limited**.

As stated in the [`LICENSE`](LICENSE), this is a **closed, proprietary research project**. 

### Pull Requests and Issues
- **Pull Requests:** We are **not accepting** external contributions, feature requests, or bug fixes. Any pull requests opened will be closed without review.
- **Issues:** The issue tracker is provided purely for informational purposes or critical academic inquiries. We do not guarantee a response or resolution to any opened issues.

### Forking and Usage
Please refer to the `LICENSE` and `DISCLAIMER.md` files for explicit details on what is and is not permitted regarding the use, reproduction, and study of this code. 

If you are an authorised researcher at Gati Shakti Vishwavidyalaya or an authorised representative of Encap Technologies India Private Limited, please follow your internal protocols for accessing and referencing this work.
