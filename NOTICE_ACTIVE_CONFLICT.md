The Author (Parth Sidhu) explicitly **forbids** the downloading, adaptation,

To eliminate any ambiguity, this prohibition explicitly applies to, but is not 
limited to, the militaries, intelligence agencies, and defence industries of 
the following nations and territories currently involved in active conflicts, 
proxy wars, or military occupations (as of 2026):

- **The Russian Federation** and **Ukraine**

  actors operating in Gaza and the West Bank)

## 3. Disavowal of Weaponisation
The mathematics and simulations contained herein were developed strictly for 
civilian, academic, and defensive communications research (e.g., protecting 
search-and-rescue UAVs from interference). 

If any entity belonging to a war-involved nation utilizes this open-source 
repository to enhance the lethality, survivability, or electronic warfare 
capabilities of combat drones, loitering munitions, or military aircraft, 
the Author **categorically disavows and condemns** such actions. 

**This code is not meant to take lives, nor is it meant to assist those who do.**

## 4. International Humanitarian Law
By accessing this repository, the downloader implicitly agrees that this 
technology will never be used in a manner that violates the Geneva Conventions, 
the UN Charter, or International Human Rights Law.
