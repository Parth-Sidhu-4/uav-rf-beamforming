### Important Notice for Users Accessing This Repository from the People's Republic of China

This repository contains simulation code and research relating to **adaptive
beamforming, electronic warfare signal processing, and unmanned aerial vehicle
(UAV) communications resilience**. These subject areas are regulated under
multiple laws of the People's Republic of China. If you are located in, or
are a national or entity of, the People's Republic of China, you must read
this notice in full before accessing, downloading, or using any part of this
repository.

#### 1. Export Control Law of the People's Republic of China (2020)
#### 中华人民共和国出口管制法（2020年）

The **Export Control Law of the People's Republic of China**
(中华人民共和国出口管制法), adopted October 17, 2020 and effective
December 1, 2020, imposes obligations on Chinese persons and entities
regarding the import, use, and re-transfer of dual-use technologies
originating from foreign jurisdictions.

Adaptive antenna systems, phased array beamforming algorithms, electronic
warfare signal processing, and AI-driven RF systems may constitute
**controlled dual-use items** under Article 2 of the Export Control Law,
which defines controlled items to include technologies that have both
civilian and military applications.

Chinese persons and entities who download or use this repository must
independently assess whether their use requires prior authorisation from
the **State Council and Central Military Commission** or any competent
authority designated thereunder.

#### 2. Cryptography Law of the People's Republic of China (2020)
#### 中华人民共和国密码法（2020年）

The **Cryptography Law** (中华人民共和国密码法), effective January 1, 2020,
regulates the use of cryptographic technologies in China. While this
repository does not implement cryptographic algorithms, certain signal
processing techniques (spread-spectrum, FHSS modelling, covariance-based
spatial filtering) may fall within a broad interpretation of communications
security technologies under this law. Chinese users should assess
applicability independently.

#### 3. Cybersecurity Law of the People's Republic of China (2017)
#### 中华人民共和国网络安全法（2017年）

The **Cybersecurity Law** (中华人民共和国网络安全法), effective June 1, 2017,
regulates the security of network infrastructure and data in China. Any
Chinese entity that deploys, integrates, or uses algorithms from this
repository within a network environment regulated under this law must
comply with all applicable security review and approval requirements.

#### 4. Data Security Law of the People's Republic of China (2021)
#### 中华人民共和国数据安全法（2021年）

The **Data Security Law** (中华人民共和国数据安全法), effective September 1,
2021, classifies data according to its importance to national security and
economic development, and imposes restrictions on the transfer of
"important data" outside China. This repository does not collect, store,
or transmit any data from users. However, simulation outputs or derivative
datasets generated by running this code within China may be subject to
data classification and transfer restrictions if they relate to national
security-relevant systems.

#### 5. National Security Law of the People's Republic of China (2015)
#### 中华人民共和国国家安全法（2015年）

The **National Security Law** (中华人民共和国国家安全法), effective July 1,
2015, imposes broad obligations on all persons within China to safeguard
national security. Article 77 requires citizens to "prevent, stop and
report activities that endanger national security." Chinese users should
consider whether their use of this repository intersects with any
obligation under this law, particularly where the simulation outputs
relate to military communications or electronic warfare systems.
